How It Works

This site was designed specifically to show you a random dog available for adoption in the hopes that those looking for a new pet will go into their search with an open mind and maybe find someone unexpected and just as lovable as any other dog.

  1. Enter your zip code above.
  2. The site will show you a dog available near you within 50 miles of your zip code.
  3. Clicking on the dog's name will take you to its Petfinder page for more information.
