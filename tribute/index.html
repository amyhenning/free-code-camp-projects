  • SA 32: Númenor founded
  • SA 600: Númenórean ships return to Middle-Earth
  • SA 1075: Tar-Ancalimë becomes the first Ruling Queen
  • SA 2251: Division amongst the Númenóreans begins
  • SA 2350: Pelargir, haven of the Faithful, is built
  • SA 3255: Ar-Pharazôn, the last king of Númenor, usurps the throne
  • SA 3319: Ar-Pharazôn sails to Valinor; fall of Númenor
"...so bright was Rothinzil that even at morning Men could see it glimmering in the West, and in the cloudless night it shone alone, for now other star could stand beside it. And setting their course towards it the Edain came at last over leagues of sea and saw afar the land that was prepared for them, Andor, the Land of Gift, shimmering in a golden haze. Then they went up out of the sea and found a country fair and fruitful, and they were glad. And they called that land Elenna, which is Starwards; but also Anadûnê, which is Westernesse, Númenórë in the High Eldarin tongue."

- The Silmarillion, Akallabêth

For a more comprehensive history of Númenor, refer to Unfinished Tales of Númenor and Middle-Earth and Appendix B of The Lord of the Rings, both by J.R.R. Tolkien. You also can read more at The Encyclopedia of Arda.
